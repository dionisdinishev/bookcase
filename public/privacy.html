Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we store your name, email, and profile picture to create your account. We also store the books you add to your shelf and your reading activity.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the Bookcase app experience — tracking your reading, displaying stats, and managing your bookshelf. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Third-party services

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage, and the Google Books API for book search.

Data deletion

You can remove individual books from your shelf at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.
